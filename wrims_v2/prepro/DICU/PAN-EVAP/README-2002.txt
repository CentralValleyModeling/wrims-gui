Only available pan evaporation data for at/near the delta area is
at Manteca per Rich Cocke of Central District.  Received copy of
data and updated from Oct 1992 through Sep 1997. (SL 12/1/97) (HR 12/29/98)
Rich Cocke retired at the end of 2002.  Data are now available from

Marc Anderson (marcla@water.ca.gov) of Central District.  Data for 1990-2002
were recieved, and this file was updated through the end of 2002.  Data for 
January-September of 2002 were used for January-September of 2003. Errors in 
water year dates for previous years were corrected.
(Jamie Anderson 1/14/03)
