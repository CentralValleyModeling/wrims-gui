Manteca Pan evaporation data were updated for CALENDER YEAR 2003 based on
data from Kim Rosmaier (krosmaie@water.ca.gov) at DWR Central District.  Historical data 
were used for water year 2003 (Oct 2002 through Sep 2003) and Oct 2003-Dec 2003. 
Monthly average pan evaporation was calculated from CIMIS ETo for Jan 2004 through
Dec 2005.  Data for April 2002 and December 2002 were updated with revised values
provided by Kim Rosmaier.
(Michael Mierzwa 1/20/04)  
Updates for 2003 were merged with the 2002 Pan Evaporation file created by Jamie Anderson.
Comments were added to indicate data sources and use of fill data.
(Jamie Anderson 1/20/04)

Data for 1990-2002 were received from Marc Anderson (marcla@water.ca.gov) of Central District, 
and this file was updated through the end of CALENDAR YEAR 2002.  Data for 
January-September of 2002 were used for January-September of 2003. Errors in 
water year dates for previous years were corrected.
(Jamie Anderson 1/14/03)
